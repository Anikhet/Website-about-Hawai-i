Pros and Cons of My Hawaii Travel Website

Pros

  • Comprehensive Content: My website covers a wide range of topics related to Hawaii travel, from history and culture to accommodations and activities. This makes it a one-stop-shop for travelers looking for information about Hawaii.
  • Visual Appeal: My decision for including images, especially for the food and dining section, adds visual appeal to the website and can help engage visitors.
  • User-Friendly Navigation: Having separate pages for different categories in my website (e.g., Destinations, Things to Do, Travel Guide) makes it easy for users to find the information they need.
  • Detailed Activity Information: Providing details about various activities, such as snorkeling, surfing, and whale watching, can help travelers plan their trip and decide what they want to do in Hawaii.
  • Flip images was used in Activites
  • Maps were used in the Food section

Cons

  • Limited Destinations: Currently, the website only mentions Maui and Oahu. Including more destinations within Hawaii could provide a more comprehensive guide for travelers.
  • Lack of Interactive Features: Adding interactive features, such as maps or a search function, could enhance the user experience and make it easier for visitors to find specific information.
  • No Personalization: Offering personalized recommendations or itineraries based on user preferences or interests could make the website more engaging and useful for individual travelers.
