History of Hawai'i

Hawaii's history is marked by the arrival of Polynesian navigators, the establishment of a complex society and monarchy, the arrival of European explorers and missionaries, the overthrow of the monarchy in 1893, and its annexation by the United States in 1898. Hawaii became the 50th state in 1959.
